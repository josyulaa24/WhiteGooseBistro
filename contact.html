ABOUT US

White Goose Bistro is an international restaurant that provides Indian, Italian, and Mexican food. We have affordable prices with variety in our food, and add new items to the menu every month! We started in 1974 to promote different types of food that you may not have had before. Some of our best items include:

  • Fettucine Pasta
  • Butter Paneer
  • Samosa
  • Birria Bowl

Stop by to try our rotating meals at your nearest location!
